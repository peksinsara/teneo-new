Studio History

Organizations are often unaware of the responsibilities of all management to IT strategy. In order to successfully execute a business plan and business strategy, IT budget and support must be aligned with business objectives. Our expert team of certified IT auditors, taking into account the findings on the one hand and best practices on the other, creates customized and accurate recommendations on how to minimize and mitigate identified risks, while the overall goal is to achieve perfect alignment of IT strategies with business goals. Following the same mission, our team of certified web developers also provides a quality web development service with a multitude of satisfied clients and successfully completed projects. Teneo as a Microsoft Partner allows us to provide you with the best experience and solution with Dynamics NAV and Business Central for your business.
