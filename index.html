Studio History

Organizations are often unaware of the responsibilities of all management to IT strategy. In order to successfully execute a business plan and business strategy, IT budget and support must be aligned with business objectives. Our expert team of certified IT auditors, taking into account the findings on the one hand and best practices on the other, creates customized and accurate recommendations on how to minimize and mitigate identified risks, while the overall goal is to achieve perfect alignment of IT strategies with business goals. Following the same mission, our team of certified web developers also provides a quality web development service with a multitude of satisfied clients and successfully completed projects.

IT Revision

Through these projects, procedures have been established to manage one of the most important information systems in Bosnia and Herzegovina. The audit of the IT infrastructure has established the need for a secondary location that will serve for Disaster Recovery and ensure the continuity of business functions in case of unforeseen downtime due to disaster, and this project is currently being implemented.

Expert Analysis

The engagements included an overview and manner of information system management as well as an analysis of the risks associated with IT functions. Comprehensive analyzes and risk identification were performed.
